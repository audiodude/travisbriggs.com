_I'm trying desperately to resist the urge to write this entire website as a choose-your-own adventure game. Must, resist._

As you enter the garden, you can see a bunch of [[music/best albums in the universe|records]] for sale, or at least on display. You also hear [[meta/danger-third-rail|a band]] playing somewhere inside. It's impossible to miss the huge amount of power extension cords and other wires darting through the gate, laying on the ground, and even going up over the walls of the garden. Whatever is going on here requires plenty of electricity it would seem.

The garden has grown quite a bit since it started, and you notice that similar ideas tend to cluster together naturally. If you follow the sound of the music, you'll find the [[music/recording-setup|recording setup]] sitting near thoughts about [[music/genre-thoughts|musical genres]] and the [[music/highs-and-lows-musician|emotional rollercoaster]] that comes with making music. The electrical cables lead you to another area where [[technology/javascript-typescript|programming languages]] are scattered around [[technology/wp-1.0|Wikipedia projects]] and various [[technology/codecov|development tools]]. 

Wandering further, you discover a quieter section devoted to [[philosophy/philosophical-beliefs|philosophical musings]], where [[philosophy/atheism|atheism]] sits comfortably next to [[philosophy/Zen|Zen thoughts]] and reflections on [[philosophy/excited|getting excited about things]]. Some [[projects/Qwobl|software projects]] live in their own little corner, along with tools like [[projects/taper|taper]] and notes about [[projects/anki|learning systems]].

If you're curious about how this whole thing works, you can explore the [[principles/digital-gardening-principles|principles behind digital gardening]] and thoughts on [[principles/digital-garden-itself|this particular garden]]. The [[people/Abby|people]] in the gardener's life get their own space too, though those notes tend to be more personal and less public.

You also see what looks like a disheveled madman rambling to himself about [[blog|blogging]]. There's a live digital display telling you about things that are going on right [[now]]. A [[writings|scrappy-looking journal]] is open on a simple table, with some handwritten poetry and the like. In the corner of the garden, there is a large smelly [[meta/compost|compost pile]] where old ideas go to decompose.

The garden maintenance happens in the [[meta/about|background]] - you can find [[meta/plans|plans]] for improvements and [[meta/comments|systems for feedback]]. Sometimes you'll stumble across [[meta/draft|draft ideas]] lying around, works in progress that aren't quite ready for prime time.

This whole place follows a few core ideas: learning in public, growing continuously, and connecting thoughts contextually rather than chronologically. Nothing is ever truly finished here - it's all evolving, always changing, like any living garden should be. You can wander wherever your curiosity takes you.
